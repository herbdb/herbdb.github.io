I am an Associate Professor at the Computer Network Information Center, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on distributed computing, cloud networks, and next-generation Internet protocols. I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University and previously worked as an R&D Technical Expert at Sangfor Technologies.
