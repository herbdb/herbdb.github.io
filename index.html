Research Fields and Directions

Our research encompasses a wide range of advanced network technologies. We primarily focus on four key areas: （1）User Equipment and Field Devices, （2）Ubiquitous Access Networks, （3）Backbone and Transport Networks, and （4）Digital Brain and Intelligence, but not limited to.

User Equipment and Field Device

Developing advanced smart devices, IoT sensors, and field equipment for efficient data collection and edge processing.

Ubiquitous Access Networks

Ensuring seamless, anywhere-anytime connectivity through integrated wireless, cellular, and broadband access technologies.

Backbone and Transport Networks

Building high-capacity, ultra-reliable core network infrastructures and optical transport systems for massive data transmission.

Digital Brain and Intelligence

Integrating artificial intelligence and big data analytics to create smart, autonomous, and self-optimizing network control systems.
