About Us

Leading the Future of Advanced Network Research

Laboratory Overview

Fanlab (Future Advanced Network Lab) is a dynamic and vibrant academic research institution dedicated to pursuing excellence in advanced network research. Since our establishment, we have been committed to innovation, rigor, and excellence, advancing scientific knowledge and technological applications in this field.

Core Mission

Promote cutting-edge scientific research, cultivate high-quality researchers, translate research outcomes into practical applications, and make significant contributions to technological progress in our society.

Laboratory History

Founded in 2020 as part of Southeast University, Fanlab has grown into an excellent team comprising senior researchers and young scientists. We have established long-term collaborative relationships with numerous leading research institutions domestically and internationally.

Research Facilities

  • 💻 Advanced computing workstations and servers
  • 🔬 State-of-the-art laboratory equipment and instruments
  • 📚 Comprehensive academic literature and databases
  • 🌐 High-speed networking and cloud computing resources
