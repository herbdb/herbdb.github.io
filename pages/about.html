About FanLab

FanLab (Future Advanced Network Lab) is a newly established, dynamic academic research institution dedicated to pursuing excellence in advanced network research. As we embark on this exciting journey, we are deeply committed to innovation, rigor, and excellence, aiming to advance scientific knowledge and pioneer breakthrough technological applications in the field.
