Current Directions

6G Intelligent Wireless Communication Networks

Focusing on Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC), wireless resource allocation, system performance optimization, and high-precision wireless positioning.

URLLC for UAV and Industrial IoT

Developing Ultra-Reliable and Low-Latency Communications (URLLC) tailored for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT).

Wi-Fi Protocols & Multi-AP Coordination

Researching advanced Wi-Fi protocols and multi-access point coordination mechanisms to achieve seamless, high-throughput, and interference-free local connectivity.

AI Infrastructure Architecture & Protocols

Designing robust architecture and protocols for AI infrastructure, including advanced load balancing, congestion control, and adaptive routing mechanisms.

Programmable Data Planes in SDN & DCN

Exploring programmable data planes, in-network computation, and in-band telemetry within Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and Data Center Networks (DCN).

Satellite & Space-Air-Ground Networks

Designing low-earth orbit (LEO) satellite constellations and space-air-ground integrated networks to achieve seamless global coverage and connectivity.
