Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to visit Fanlab?

Please contact our Academic Exchange office via email with your background and research interests. We'll respond within two weeks.

Do you accept internship applications?

Yes, we welcome talented students and researchers. Please send your CV and research proposal to jobs@fanlab.seu.edu.cn.

How can we become a collaborative research partner?

Please contact our International Cooperation department to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

What are your office hours?

We operate Monday through Friday, 9:00AM - 5:00PM. For urgent matters, please call our 24-hour emergency line.
