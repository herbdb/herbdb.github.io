2026-04-22 Conference

IEEE GIIS 2026 Hosted by Fanlab in Nanjing

Fanlab is proud to host the IEEE Global Information Infrastructure and Networking Symposium (GIIS) 2026, taking place in Nanjing from April 22 to April 24.

The conference will bring together leading researchers, scholars, and industry experts to discuss the latest advancements in information infrastructure and networking. We invite all practitioners to join us for this exciting event to share innovative ideas and foster international collaborations.

For more details regarding the schedule, keynote speakers, and registration, please visit our official conference website.

2026-03-15 Call for Papers

Call for Papers: The 30th ACM SIGOPS ChinaSys in Xi'an

We warmly welcome submissions for the 30th ACM SIGOPS ChinaSys, which will be held in the historic city of Xi'an from May 30 to May 31, 2026.

ACM SIGOPS ChinaSys is a premier academic event dedicated to gathering researchers and practitioners in the field of computer systems. It provides an excellent platform to present cutting-edge research, exchange ideas, and explore future trends in systems research.

We encourage researchers, faculty members, and students to submit their latest research findings. We look forward to seeing you in Xi'an for insightful discussions and networking opportunities!
